# Privacy Policy — SuperBrushKids
**Developer:** BrushHero Labs  
**Contact:** 	avyani.studio25@gmail.com  
**Effective date:** 03/05/2026

SuperBrushKids is designed for families and children. We take privacy seriously and aim to collect as little data as possible.

## Summary
- We do **not** require accounts or logins.
- Brushing profiles and session history are stored **locally on your device**.
- Purchases are handled by **Google Play / Apple** and may be managed via **RevenueCat** to enable “restore purchases.”
- We do not sell personal information.

## Information We Collect
### 1) Information you enter in the app
- Child profile nickname (e.g., “Ava”) and avatar settings (e.g., color)
- Brushing session history (e.g., session date/time, episode shown, completion status, stars earned)

This information is stored **on your device** to make the app work.

### 2) Purchase-related information (if you buy Lifetime Unlock)
If you choose to purchase, the transaction is processed by Google Play (Android) or Apple (iOS). We do not receive your payment card details.

To support purchase validation and “Restore Purchases,” we may use RevenueCat, which may process purchase receipts and device/app identifiers needed to confirm your entitlement (Premium access).

## How We Use Information
We use information only to:
- Create and display child profiles
- Track brushing sessions, stars, and streaks
- Enable and restore premium access after purchase

## Data Storage & Retention
- Brushing data and profiles are stored **locally on your device**.
- If you delete the app, local data may be removed by your operating system.
- We do not maintain a separate user database for app usage data.

## Sharing of Information
We do not sell or rent personal information. We share information only with:
- **Google Play / Apple** for payment processing
- **RevenueCat** (if enabled) for purchase management (entitlements and restore)

## Children’s Privacy
SuperBrushKids is intended for use by children with parental guidance.
- We do not require children to provide real names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location.
- We do not show targeted advertising.
- Purchases should be completed by a parent/guardian.

## Security
We use reasonable safeguards within the app; however, no method of storage is 100% secure. Because most data stays on your device, you control it.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date above.
